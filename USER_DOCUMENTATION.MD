# Utilisation Calculator - User Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Sign In or Sign Up

When you first visit the application:

- **New users**: Click "Sign Up" and create an account with your email and password
- **Returning users**: Click "Sign In" and enter your existing credentials

### 2. Profile Setup

After authentication, the system automatically initializes your profile with:

- Your full name from Clerk
- Your preferred language
- Your unique Clerk ID

## Using the Calculator

### 3. Calculate Your Utilisation

To track your performance:

1. **Enter your tracking period**:
   - Select start date
   - Select end date

2. **Add your hours**:
   - Total hours worked
   - Billable hours (time charged to clients)
   - Target utilisation percentage

3. **Get results**:
   - Click "Calculate"
   - View your actual percentage
   - See if you met your target

### 4. Review Your History

- Access "Utilisation History" to view all previous calculations
- Compare performance across different periods
- Track your utilisation trends over time
- Delete any incorrect entries as needed
